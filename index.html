About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Atmospheric Science at Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand. My research focuses on understanding hemispheric asymmetry in simulating atmospheric blocking and heat extremes under a warming planet.

With a strong foundation in numerical modeling, AI/ML, Atmospheric and Climate Science, I am committed to addressing critical questions in a rapidly changing climate and intensifying extreme weather events. My work aims to advance our understanding of these phenomena and contribute to the development of robust future projections for a rapidly changing world.
